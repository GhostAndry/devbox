# 🚀 DevBox

> A fast, offline-first developer toolbox that lives in your browser.

DevBox is an all-in-one collection of utilities for developers, system administrators, DevOps engineers, and anyone who spends too much time opening 15 different websites just to generate a UUID, format JSON, or calculate a chmod value.

Everything runs **client-side**. No backend. No accounts. No tracking. No ads.

## 🔒 Privacy

DevBox is designed to be privacy-first.

* No account required
* No analytics (planned)
* No tracking
* No cookies (except those strictly required by the browser)
* Everything runs locally whenever possible

Your data never leaves your device unless a specific online tool (such as DNS or WHOIS lookup) requires a network request.
